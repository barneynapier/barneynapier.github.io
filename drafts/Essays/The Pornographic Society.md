Pleasure and happiness are two very different things, and porn is the clearest example of how the two often contradict each other.  
  
Porn has many negative effects in the long term  
* Increased likelihood of sexual violence  
* Dehumanisation of women  
* Dopamine addiction - More likely to become addicted to other things  
* De-sensitisation to real sex, leading to sexual dysfunction  
* Wrong view on relationships, hence worse relationships  
  
We also know about some factors that cause the effects of porn to be more impactful  
* Earlier exposure - When we are younger our brains have more neuroplasticity, so they adapt faster to the input they are given. If that input is porn then it can have very bad impacts in the long term  
* More regular exposure
