# Cold Showers Research  
  
Metabolism  
By releasing more norepinephrine into the body, cold showers force additional blood to flow to the muscles. Hence you will burn more calories as your muscles are more activated.  
  
Endorphins & Depression  
The below paper finds that taking regular cold showers regularly reduces symptoms of depression by inducing the release of more noradrenaline (/norepinephrine), a drug that has been used to treat ADHD, depression and low blood pressure.  
Adapted cold shower as a potential treatment for depression  
  
Cardiovascular  
Cold showers cause your body to pump blood around faster in order to get heat to the colder elements of the body. This helps keep the cardiovascular system healthy by pushing it a little further than it would usually go, especially during a shower.  
  
Willpower & Resilience  
This one has less academic evidence to support it, but I believe that subjecting yourself to an unpleasant sensation every day will build up the amount of willpower you have as well as get you used to being uncomfortable. The more comfortable you become when dealing with discomfort the more resilient you will be and the more ready to deal with stress.

Immune System  
Cold showers improve your immune system, you get less little niggling illnesses etc.
