# The Unnatural Life  
  
* We have evolved to an environment that we no longer live in  
* Over time we will adjust to our new environment  
* But for our lifetime - and many lifetimes to come - that will not happen  
* So we should live the natural life The unnatural life  
    * Sitting at a desk all day  
    * Clothing that shields us from nature  
    * Everything available at the click of a button  
* Human being no longer do what they were made for Tech addiction  
    * We are becoming addicted to social media and technology. Mindlessly scrolling for hours on end, thinking its good for us because it "keeps us connected with people."  
    * Often connection isnt the issue, addiction is. And the way technology is changing our brains is not good for our focus, productivity and creativity.
