Heuristics of Happiness  
*   
* Affective Forecasting - We get intensity and duration wrong of how good something will make us feel  
*   
* Reference Point Bias - We always choose reference points that make us feel inferior  
*   
* [Hedonic Adaptation](app://obsidian.md/Hedonic%20Adaptation) (rule of 7) - We get used to good things fast
