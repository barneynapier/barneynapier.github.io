Making it a habit of doing what failures dont like to do  
  
What are those things?  
* Things that people dont like doing  
* You probably wont like doing these things either  
  
How can you make yourself do them?  
* Have a strong purpose for doing them  
  
Why is habit important?  
* "People form habits and habits form futures"  
  
What purpose?  
* Something practical  
* Something not logical  
So overall: Make it a habit to do things you don't want to do. It will reap rewards
