Argument for quality > quantity of communication  
* In the present day we are constantly in contact with all those we wish to be. We trust and know that nobody in our phone book will reply any later than a day.  
* As a result, correspondence with our friends and family is similar to a constant stream of excrement.  
* We do not write in full sentences, nor do we have to think about the structure of what we are saying.  
* Things simply enter our heads and go straight into a message. It is like we are drip feeding the most important individuals in our lives with mixed barrage of disorganised brain-farts, topical memes and cat videos.  
* Surely our closest acquaintances deserve better than this?  
* Things didn’t always used to be this way. It is technology which has facilitated our 250 character conversations.
