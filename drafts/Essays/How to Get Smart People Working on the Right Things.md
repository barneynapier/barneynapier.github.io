# How to Get Smart People Working on the Right Things  
  
I've spent all my adult life (so far) working in Finance.  
One thing that shocked me (morally), is the number of really smart people who have forgone careers in fields that add more value to society.

It comes in part from the desire of companies within finance to have 'diversity of thought' among their employees. In a team of 10, the company does not want 10 graduates of Economics. Instead they want some scientists, some language graduates and some mathematicians.  
  
As a result they are taking high quality talent out of other industries. Not intentionally mind you, but the simple fact that financial services is a typically high paying industry will draw a young scientist to work at a bank instead of in a research facility.

In part, it is because the value creation in finance is immediate. Whereas working on problems to improve society has a delayed payoff that could easily be beyond the lifetime of those working in the field. That means the only ones that will commit to a career of research (or a similar path) are those with the moral compass to do so.
