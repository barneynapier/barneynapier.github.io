# How I Read  
  
A constantly updating process.  
  
Fiction  
* Read on Kindle, highlight anything notable  
* Summarise key ideas of book from memory, written in kindle throughout the book too  
* Use script to map to markdown (use loc based indexing)  
* Add clippings to notes  
  
Non-Fiction  
Read the physical book, take notes in the margin, summarise chapters  
* Familiarise - Read book summary/notes to get idea of the concept  
* Shallow read - Read quickly, understand core ideas and take notes  
* (Deep read) - Read (parts) in detail, take notes and summarise chapters  
* Notes - Aggregate chapter notes into markdown, link to other notes and ideas  
  
Add any higher level thoughts to my notes after I have written them up
