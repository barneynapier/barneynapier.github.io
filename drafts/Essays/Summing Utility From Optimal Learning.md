# Summing Utility From Optimal Learning  
  
* Most of economics assumes that marginal returns and utilities are diminishing  
* In reality, most things get better at first, then worse (in terms of marginal benefits)  
* Much like the learning curve, where you struggle to learn the basics, then you begin to learn faster until you have learned a lot, then it becomes harder to learn the final bits  
* We must be able to make an economic model that incorporates the shape of the learning curve as a marginal utility curve / S shaped curve
