# Learning from Lower belts  
  
BJJ is a multi-faceted discipline  
Generally there is not a ‘better’ or ‘worse’ in Jiu jitsu when you are similar in belt level  
For example, as a blue belt I was a good passer but my guard wasn’t that good  
White belts could and would pass my guard and submit me  
If you keep hold of your ego, you can never learn from these situations because you are too stuck in the idea that you are ‘better’ then this lower belt at jiu jitsu  
But maybe they are good for their level in a specific situation  
If you can learn from these people, you open yourself up to faster improvement
